Founder's Vision
The founding vision of ICST is rooted in the belief that higher education should be a transformative force capable of breaking down socio-economic barriers. ICST was established with the explicit purpose of providing job-oriented higher education for those with limited financial means, creating an environment where every aspiring student, regardless of their economic background, can access quality education and embark on a journey of enfranchisement.

The founder envisioned a university with inclusivity that ensures students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds have equal opportunities to pursue higher education.

The founder is believing in the power of community support. ICST actively engages with local communities, businesses, and philanthropic organizations to establish partnerships that further support our mission.

To create a conducive learning environment, the founder prioritized the establishment of physical and infrastructure facilities with cutting-edge technology, architectural marvel, and esthetic appeal of a world-class learning environment.

The founder’s vision is a commitment to social responsibility too. ICST engages in initiatives that address societal challenges, contributing to the betterment of communities and emphasizing the role of higher education in creating positive social change, and contributing towards human resources development of our country.

About ICST

The International Campus of Science & Technology (ICST) is dedicated to providing world-class education and fostering innovation in science and technology. Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to excel in a rapidly changing world. At ICST, we believe in holistic development, hands-on learning, and a global perspective.

Join us to experience a vibrant campus life, cutting-edge research, and a supportive community that nurtures your ambitions.

Academic Programs

HDIT
Higher Diploma in Information Technology

  • Software Development
  • Networking
  • Database Management
Program Details:
  • Duration: 2 Years (Full Time)
  • Eligibility: A/L or equivalent
  • Career Paths: After topup (Software Engineer, Network Engineer, cyber Securityre, AI Engineer)
  • Highlights: Industry projects, internship, modern labs

HDQS
Higher Diploma in Quantity Surveying

  • Construction Management
  • Cost Estimation
  • Project Planning
Program Details:
  • Duration: 2 Years (Full Time)
  • Eligibility: A/L or equivalent
  • Career Paths: Quantity Surveyor, Cost Consultant, Site Manager
  • Highlights: Field visits, industry tie-ups, project-based learning

ACCA
Association of Chartered Certified Accountants

  • Financial Accounting
  • Management Accounting
  • Taxation
Program Details:
  • Duration: 2-3 Years (Flexible)
  • Eligibility: A/L or equivalent
  • Career Paths: Chartered Accountant, Auditor, Finance Manager
  • Highlights: Global recognition, exam support, internship

BAIT
Bachelor of Applied in Information Technology

  • AI
  • IT Project Management
  • Cyber Security
Program Details:
  • Duration: 3 Years (Full Time)
  • Eligibility: HDIT or A/L with IT background
  • Career Paths: IT Consultant, AI Engineer, Security Analyst
  • Highlights: Research projects, industry mentors, global exposure
